Saves

Shoppers can save items to lists while in-store, then be notified when those items are in-stock or on sale.

Conversions

Target customer sements with personalised deals or recommendations, and reduce the time your staff needs to dedicate to each customer.

Retention

Follow up with your customers through emails and digital receipts with special deals and coupons to keep them coming back.

Retargeting

If a customer browses in-store, but chooses not to buy, retarget them with 0-party data from their saves and personalised offers based on their browsing history.
